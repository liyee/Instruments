Vehicle loaded hydraulic puller

Source：Tarzan Instruments  Time：2019-07-23
  • Introduction
  • Drawings
  • Parameters
Vehicle loaded hydraulic puller
● Used to disassemble transmission components such as bearings, clutches, bushings, gears, etc.
● With 25mm horizontal adjustment, 5 degree vertical adjustment can be well aligned accurately
● Fully automatic control, all the drawing work can be completed through one button box
●Using an electric pump station, the hydraulic cylinder is stacked to the full stroke for 30 seconds.
●The hydraulic cylinder adopts double action mode to shorten the time of the target and speed up the work efficiency.
●The claw hook is made of forged steel, which is durable and durable.
● Solenoid valve controls the hook to open the table, shortening the adjustment time
●Slip-proof design to prevent the claw hook from slipping or jumping off the workpiece
● Self-centering, claw hooks can be opened and closed at the same time
●The car has two universal wheels, which can be self-locking.
●Use 220V-1.1Kw electric hydraulic pump station as power source
