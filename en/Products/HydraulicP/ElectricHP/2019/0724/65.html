Electric hydraulic pump
Product introduction:
Ningbo Instruments Mechanics electric hydraulic pump is designed for a variety of industrial production applications, can also be directly equipped with the corresponding equipment for pushing, pulling, expanding, clamping, bending, jacking, extrusion and other operations.

Product parameters:
 Working pressure 70MPa
 Available oil quantity 2-200 L
 High pressure flow 0.6-7.2 L/min
 High-performance aluminum motor for fast heat dissipation and long-term use.
 Performance is industrial grade and is particularly suitable as a power source for production lines.
 Various sizes of fuel tanks (30-200 liters) can be customized to meet customers' various oil requirements.
 The ED release valve series is suitable for punching and shearing.
 TheTIS-22M,TID-22M are silent series, which have improved performance and extended service life due to the use of DC motors.
