High pressure hydraulic hand pump

The high pressure hydraulic hand pump has single oil passage and double oil passage, and is mainly used for supporting the use of ultra high pressure oil cylinder, hydraulic nut and hydraulic bolt tensioner.
● High working pressure and easy to use
● Made of aluminum-titanium alloy material, light weight, high strength, corrosion resistance, impact resistance, suitable for various harsh environments
● External load relief valve
● Built-in relief valve to prevent overload
● Two-speed operation, high efficiency, large fuel tank capacity
● Directional valve can be added for operating double-acting hydraulic cylinder
