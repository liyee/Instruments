Hydraulic hand pump

Source：Tarzan Instruments  Time：2019-07-24
  • Introduction
  • Drawings
  • Parameters
Ningbo Instruments Mechanics Co., Ltd offers a wide range of hydraulic hand pump as a reliable power source for hydraulic tools. Our SBP series hydraulic hand pump are designed with the following features:

 Compact, light-weight, easy-to-carry design, pump body is made of hard plastic;
 Large oil capacity, and oil tank can be customized for specific hydraulic tools;
 Hand operated, convenient, especially can be widely used in wild and places where electricity, pneumatic power supply are unavailable;
 Safety pressure release valve to protect over-pressuring, and overload;
 Standard parts such as pressure gauge, NPT3/8 couplers, gauge saddles, hydraulic hose are available to choose.
