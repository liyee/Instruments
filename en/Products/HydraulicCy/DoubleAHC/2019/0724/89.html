Double Acting Aluminum Hydraulic Cylinders

NINGBO INSTRUMENTS MECHANICS CO., LTD Double Acting Aluminum Hydraulic Cylinders are designed with aluminum alloy, light weight, very convenient to carry, yet very durable, and reliable. Up to 50% lighter than equivalent tonnage steel cylinders. Double acting, oil pressure return, fast and efficient. Aluminum hydraulic jacks are used in areas such as oil and refinery, chemical, steel, shipyards, power plants, cements and mining industries, normally do not requirement large capacity over 300 tons.

Features:
Capacity: 50-150 ton
Stroke: 50 - 200 mm
Customized service available
Maximum Operating Pressure: 700 bar
 Double acting, hydraulic pressure return, fast and efficient;
 Aluminum cylinders save weight more than 50% compared to steel cylinders;
 Compact design for lower closed height and save space;
 Quality seals sustain long life working and stable performance;
  Double dust protection rings to prevent dust coming into the cylinder piston while traveling;
 All cylinders are equipped with saddles to protect the piston for side-slipping;
 Auto stop ring to avoid the cylinder piston exceed maximum stroke to protect the piston;
 All cylinders are equipped with safety valve to prevents over-loading, and over pressure;
 Standard accessories on the cylinder:　NPT 3/8 Couplings, carrying handles, saddles, dust-free protection rings, safety valve
