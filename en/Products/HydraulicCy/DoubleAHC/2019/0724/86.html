 Double Acting High Tonnage Hydraulic Cylinders

NINGBO INSTRUMENTS MECHANICS CO., LTD Double Acting High Tonnage Hydraulic Cylinders are designed with high capacity and long stroke, double acting, piston oil pressure fast turn, efficient. The capacity can be customized, standard capacity is now up to 1000 Ton. Double Acting High Tonnage Hydraulic Jack is widely used in heavy duty construction area, oil platform, crane, cone crusher, power plant, grind mill, equipment and machinery lifting, supporting, installing, maintenance job. We did many bridge constructions projects in China, and in North Europe, South America.

Features:
Capacity: 50-1000 ton
Stroke: 50 - 300 mm
Customized service available
Maximum Operating Pressure: 700 bar
 Double acting, oil pressure return;
 Two base mounting holes, carrying shackles are provided with all models;
 High capacity, long stroke design for heavy duty;
 Quality seals sustain long life working and stable performance;
 Industrial hard chrome plated ram prevents scratching and corrosion;
 High strength steel construction from gun drilled solid bar;
 External thread to install saddles, covers, or machines for support and lift;
 Double dust protection rings to prevent dust coming into the cylinder piston while traveling;
 Stop ring to avoid the cylinder piston exceed maximum stroke to protect the piston;
 Standard saddles are available to protect the piston and prevent from side-slipping, extend working life;
 All cylinders are equipped with safety valve to prevents over-loading, and over pressure;
 Standard accessories on the cylinder:　NPT 3/8 Couplings, carrying handles, saddles, dust-free protection rings, safety valve.
