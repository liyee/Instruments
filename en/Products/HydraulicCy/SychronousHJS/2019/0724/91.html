Synchronous PLC Hydraulic Lifting System
Model number Lift points Integrated pump Accuracy (mm) Touch screen control Leveling Weighing CG Expandable
TII-SLCG 4, 8 yes 0.5,1 yes yes yes* no no
TII-ESS 4, 8, 12,24,32,64 yes 0.5,1 yes yes yes yes Yes

Features:
• Number of Lifting Points: 4, 8, 12 or 16,24, 32,64
• Accuracy Over Full Stroke: ± 0.5 mm
• Each point support up to 2,000 tons, with 64 points system, the total max lifting capacity up to 128,000 ton. Customized cylinder capacity offered.
• Maximum Operating Pressure: 700 bar
• Frequency conversion, closed hydraulic oil circuit, self-adjusting electronic pulse control
• Valve control button to averagely distribute jack loading to avoid overloading or slippery,
• Advanced hydraulic oil speed control to maintain height precision, fast speed lifting and coming back, speed controllable
• Load and stroke alarms for optimal safety
• Colored touchable screen, Microsoft Window Interface to make settings, monitor all distribution of loading and data storage and recording capabilities

Application Area
• Multi purpose: house translation/bridge construction or supporting,
• Off-shore oil plant drilling platform supporting and equipment testing.
• Antique pagoda, building leveling translation
• Crude oil Tank wielding support

Application Case
• Shanghai - Beijing High Speed Railway Yangtze River Bridge Project
• Shanghai Baosteel testing and installing
• The three Gorge Dam, hydropower plant installing
• Zhangfei Temple translation in Sichuan
