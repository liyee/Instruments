 Multi-stage Hydraulic Cylinders

NINGBO INSTRUMENTS MECHANICS CO., LTD Multi-stage Hydraulic Cylinders are designed 2 or 3 stage, allow for different steps lifting, while extended full, it can enjoy very long stroke. Multi-stage Hydraulic Cylinders closed height is among 250 to 365 mm. The stroke range is among 135 -500 mm. Capacity is from 10 ton to 300 ton. Multi-stage hydraulic jacks is very useful in long stroke requirements working conditions, multi-functional, versatile in different height and capacity working conditions.

Features:
Capacity: 10-300 ton
Stroke: 135 - 500 mm
Customized service available
Maximum Operating Pressure: 700 bar
 Single acting, spring return;
 Long stroke design for high support and lifting requirements;
 Multistage allow for different height and capacity, space conditions;
 Hardness and anti-rust surface treatment for long life and stable performance;
 Quality seals sustain long life working and stable performance;
 Dust protection ring to prevent dust coming into the cylinder piston while traveling;
 Stop ring to avoid the cylinder piston exceed maximum stroke to protect the piston;
 All cylinders are equipped with safety valve to prevents over-loading, and over pressuring;
 Standard accessories on the cylinder: NPT 3/8 Couplings, carrying handles, saddles, dust-free protection rings, safety valve.
