 Single Acting Aluminum Hydraulic Cylinders

NINGBO INSTRUMENTS MECHANICS CO., LTD Single Acting Aluminum Hydraulic Cylinders are designed with aluminum alloy, light weight, very convenient to carry, yet very durable, and reliable. Up to 50% lighter than equivalent tonnage steel cylinders. Single Acting Aluminum Hydraulic jacks are used in areas such as oil and refinery, chemical, steel, shipyards, power plants, cements and mining industries, normally do not require large capacity over 200 tons.

Features:
Capacity: 20-150 ton
Stroke: 50 - 200 mm
Customized service available
Maximum Operating Pressure: 700 bar
 Single acting, spring return;
 Two base mounting holes, carrying shackles are provided with all models;
 Aluminum cylinders save weight more than 50% weight compared to steel cylinders;
 Composite bearings prevent metal to metal contact, increasing cylinder life and resistance to side-loads of up to 10%;
 Compact design for lower closed height and save space;
 Quality seals sustain long life working and stable performance;
 Double dust protection rings to prevent dust coming into the cylinder piston while traveling;
 Standard saddles are available to protect the piston and prevent from side-slipping, extend working life;
 Auto stop ring to avoid the cylinder piston exceed maximum stroke to protect the piston;
 All cylinders are equipped with safety valve to prevents over-loading, and over pressure;
 Standard accessories on the cylinder:　NPT 3/8 Couplings, carrying handles, saddles, dust-free protection rings, safety valve.
