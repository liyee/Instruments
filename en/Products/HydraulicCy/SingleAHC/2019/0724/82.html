 Single Acting Hollow Plunger Cylinders

NINGBO INSTRUMENTS MECHANICS CO., LTD Single Acting Hollow Hydraulic Cylinders are designed with hollow central hole worked as push and pull forces to lift, support, push and pull, press job. It can be installed and used for hydraulic pullers to pull bearing and wheels. Single acting hollow hydraulic jack height can be as low as 57.5 mm, however, the capacity can reach 150 ton maximally. The stroke range is among 25-155 mm. Single Acting Hollow Hydraulic rams are commonly used in many industries, such as oil and refinery, petroleum, chemical and steel plants, power plants equivalents, installing, testing, and maintenance etc.

Features:
Capacity: 12-150 ton
Stroke: 25 - 155 mm
Customized service available
Maximum Operating Pressure: 700 bar
 Single acting, spring return;
 The hollow plunger cylinder can be used vertically for lifting and supporting, as well as horizontally for pushing and pulling work; also can be used for tensioning;
 Quality seals sustain long life working and stable performance;
 Hardness and anti-corrosion surface treatment for long life and stable performance;
 Piston external thread allow for installing on machinery or objects;
 Dust protection rings to prevent dust coming into the cylinder piston while traveling;
 Stop ring to avoid the cylinder piston exceed maximum stroke to protect the piston;
 All cylinders are equipped with safety valve to prevents over-loading, and over pressuring;
 Standard accessories on the cylinder: NPT 3/8 Couplings, carrying handles, dust-free protection rings, safety valve.
