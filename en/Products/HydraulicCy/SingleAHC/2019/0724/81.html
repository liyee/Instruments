Single Acting Hydraulic Cylinders

NINGBO INSTRUMENTS MECHANICS CO., LTD offers Single Acting Hydraulic Cylinders with capacity from 5 ton up to 100 ton, capacity and strokes can be customized by customers requests. Single acting hydraulic jacks are widely applied in areas where lifting or supporting capacity is relatively small, stroke can be as long as 350 mm, or even longer.

Features:
Capacity: 5-100 ton
Stroke: 25 - 356mm
Customized service available
Maximum Operating Pressure: 700 bar
 Single acting, spring return;
 High strength alloy steel for durability;
 Quality seals sustain long life working and stable performance;
 Hardness and anti-rust surface treatment for long life and stable performance;
 Double dust protection rings to prevent dust coming into the cylinder piston while traveling;
 Stop ring to avoid the cylinder piston exceed maximum stroke to protect the piston;
 All cylinders are equipped with safety valve to prevents over-loading, and over pressuring;
 Standard saddles are available to protect the piston and prevent from side-slipping, extend working life;
 External thread are provided under customers request to install saddles, covers, or machines for support and lift. Installing hole at the bottom;
 Standard accessories on the cylinder:　NPT 3/8 Couplings, carrying handles, saddles, dust-free protection rings, safety valve.
