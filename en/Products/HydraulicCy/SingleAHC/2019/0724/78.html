 Single Acting Ultra Thin High Pressure Hydraulic Cylinders

NINGBO INSTRUMENTS MECHANICS CO., LTD Single Acting Ultra Thin High Pressure Hydraulic Cylinders are designed with ultra low height, and limited stroke in confined space and working conditions. The collapsed height is 32, 35 mm, stroke is 10 mm. Working pressure can be as high as 2000 bar. The cylinders in designed with ultra low height, and ultra high pressure to make sure it can support and lift heavy objects. Single Acting Ultra Thin High Pressure Hydraulic jack can be used in very heavy objects lifting and supporting in a very limited space, such as bridge seat change, heat exchanger, power generator, wind turbine, supporting and lifting.

Features:
Capacity: 50-300 ton
Stroke: 10 mm
Customized service available
Maximum Operating Pressure: 1500, 2000 bar
 Single acting, spring return;
 Ultra thin and high precision, very useful in limited space and areas, especially in high precision machinery or equipment test and installing;
 Ultra high pressure, space saving and powerful capacity;
 Quality seals sustain long life working and stable performance;
 Quality seals sustain long life working and stable performance；
 Special surface treatment for working in restricted environment;
 Stop ring to avoid the cylinder piston exceed maximum stroke to protect the piston;
 Standard saddles are available to protect the piston and prevent from side-slipping, extend working life;
 All cylinders are equipped with safety valve to prevents over-loading, and over pressuring;
 Standard accessories on the cylinder:　G 1/4 Couplings, dust-free protection rings, safety valve.
