  Single Acting Thin Hydraulic Cylinders

Ningbo Instruments Mechanics Co., Ltd Single Acting Thin Hydraulic Cylinders are designed with low height, and relatively small stroke in confined space and working conditions. Single acting thin hydraulic cylinder height can be as low as 88 mm, however, the capacity can reach 150 ton maximally. The stroke are as high as more than 64 mm. Single Acting Thin Hydraulic jacks are commonly used in bridge seat changing, supporting and lifting work for equipment and heavy machinery in many plants and industries.

Features:
Capacity: 10-150 ton
Stroke: 40 - 64 mm
Customized service available
Maximum Operating Pressure: 700 bar
 Single acting, spring return;
 Two base mounting holes;
 30,50,50,100 ton thin hydraulic cylinder are widely and commonly used in many areas, which can be used in confined space;
 Quality seals sustain long life working and stable performance;
 Hardness and anti-rust surface treatment for long life and stable performance;
 Dust protection ring to prevent dust coming into the cylinder piston while traveling;
 Standard saddles are available to protect the piston and prevent from side-slipping, extend working life;
 Stop ring to avoid the cylinder piston exceed maximum stroke to protect the piston from pulling out;
 All cylinders are equipped with safety valve to prevents over-loading, and over pressuring;
 Standard accessories are offered with the cylinder:　NPT 3/8 Couplings,, dust-free protection rings, safety valve.
