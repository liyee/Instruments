 Single Acting High Tonnage Locknut Hydraulic Cylinders

NINGBO INSTRUMENTS MECHANICS CO., LTD Single Acting High Tonnage Locknut Hydraulic Cylinders are designed with large capacity and locknut. Single acting low profile locknut hydraulic cylinder height can be as low as 225 mm, however, the capacity can reach 1,000 ton maximally. The stroke range is among 50-300 mm. Compared to other cylinders, locknut hydraulic jacks can support and lift, for longer time holding without the hydraulic pump. When you pump to certain height, you can remove the hydraulic pump. It is widely used in construction, big machinery and heavy equipment installing, maintenance.

Features:
Capacity: 50-1000 ton
Stroke: 50-300 mm
Customized service available
Maximum Operating Pressure: 700 bar
 Single acting, spring return; two base mounting holes;
 Carrying shackles are provided with all models;
 High capacity, up to 1,000 ton maximally;
 Quality seals sustain long life working and stable performance;
 Locknut hydraulic cylinders can sustain the pressure and capacity after removing hydraulic pump;
 Hardness and anti-rust surface treatment for long life and stable performance;
 Dust protection ring to prevent dust coming into the cylinder piston while traveling;
 Standard saddles are available to protect the piston and prevent from side-slipping, extend working life;
 Stop ring to avoid the cylinder piston exceed maximum stroke to protect the piston;
 All cylinders are equipped with safety valve to prevents over-loading, and over pressuring;
 Standard accessories on the cylinder: NPT 3/8 Couplings, carrying handles, saddles, dust-free protection rings, safety valve.
