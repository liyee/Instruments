Single Acting Low Profile Hydraulic Cylinders

NINGBO INSTRUMENTS MECHANICS CO., LTD Single Acting Low Profile Hydraulic Cylinders are designed with low height, and low stroke in confined space and limited working conditions. Single acting thin hydraulic cylinder height can be as low as 33 mm, however, the capacity can reach 150 ton maximally. The stroke range is among 6-20 mm. Single Acting Low Profile Hydraulic Jacks are commonly used in many industries, such as oil and refinery, petroleum, chemical and steel plants, power plants equivalents, installing and maintenance etc.

Features:
Capacity: 5-150 ton
Stroke: 6 - 18 mm
Customized service available
Maximum Operating Pressure: 700 bar
 Single acting, spring return;
 Two base mounting holes;
 Low profile, large capacity, space saving, useful in confine space and sites;
 Quality seals sustain long life working and stable performance;
 Hardness and anti-rust surface treatment for long life and stable performance;
 Installation holes to hold or install on machine or seats;
 Dust protection ring to prevent dust coming into the cylinder piston while traveling;
 Heat treatment on the piston to prevent from damaging, no need saddles;
 Stop ring to avoid the cylinder piston exceed maximum stroke to protect the piston;
 All cylinders are equipped with safety valve to prevents over-loading, and over pressuring;
 Standard accessories on the cylinder: NPT 3/8 Couplings, dust-free protection rings, safety valve.
