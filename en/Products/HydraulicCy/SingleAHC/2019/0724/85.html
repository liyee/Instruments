 Mechanic Screw Cylinders

NINGBO INSTRUMENTS MECHANICS CO., LTD Screw Hydraulic Cylinders are designed with screw operated, with small stroke, small capacity.  It does not require hydraulic pump to lifting or support, rather, it is operated mechanically by a screw. Screw Hydraulic Jack is very convenient and useful for precise lifting and supporting, in machinery testing, maintenance.

Features:
Capacity: 5 -50 ton
Stroke: 5-25 mm
Customized service available
Maximum Operating Pressure: 700 bar
 Compact design, mechanic operation;
 Convenient and helpful, do not need hydraulic pump;
 Screw adjusting for lifting and supporting, return;
 Ultra thin and high tonnage; no need additional power pack;
 Hardness and anti-rust surface treatment for long life and stable performance;
 It is sealed with hydraulic oil.
