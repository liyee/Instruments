Hydraulic Nut Splitter

Bolts and nuts are widely used in industrial application areas such as in the open air, explosive, or corrosive environments. After long time serving, or due to poor working condition, some are rusted, threads were damaged, which makes removal or dismantling nuts and bolts difficult, even impossible. Usually we use (gas) or high temperature to nut off, but in the conditions mentioned above, and special gas were unavailable, and sometimes even fire and gas are prohibited, such as coal storage power plants, oil pipelines and other high-pressure high-temperature piping at the nuts and bolts. Tarzan Instruments instrument hydraulic nut splitters do not require electricity, gas, no damage to the threaded bolts, simple, fast, safe, provide efficient solution to bolt and nut removal and replacements.

Tarzan Instruments International hydraulic nut splitter features:
 Spring return cylinder, easy to use on ships;
 Optional double-acting hydraulic cylinders suitable for submarine operations;
 The cutting blade can be easily adjusted in advance to cut the outside nuts at the same time prevent damage to the bolt external thread;
 Visual bolt diameter thread, easy to adjust the cutting blade;
 Integrated pressure relief valve to prevent excessive pressure;

Tarzan Instruments International hydraulic nut splitters easy manual:

Step 1. Open the hand pump oil tank cap, fill with hydraulic oil and then screw the cap tight;
Step 2. Connect hydraulic hand pump to the hydraulic nut splitter by hydraulic hose quick couplers;
Step 3. Place the hydraulic nut splitter cutting blade to the nut surface needed to remove, straighten flat;
Step 4. Operate the hydraulic hand pump, the pressure push the hydraulic nut splitter blade into nut body, when the blade goes into two thirds of the nut, slowly pump the hand pump to avoid over-cutting, damaging the bolt threads;
Step 5. When you hear a sound of nut break, it indicates that the nut has been broken open, stop pumping the pump, remove the splitter and pump.
