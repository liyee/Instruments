Large Capacity Square Drive Hydraulic Torque Wrench
( Stainless Steel Alloy )


Ningbo Instruments Mechanics Co., ltd Hydraulic torque wrench is powerful solution for high tensile bolt and nuts tightening and dismantling. The hydraulic torque wrench is ultimate tool in heavy duty work. Tarzan Instruments hydraulic torque wrench offers standard products up to 70,000 Nm, with bolt diameter size up to 125mm, and hexagon across flats up to 180mm. We have customized hydraulic torque wrench up to 160,000 Nm for the world’s biggest man made dam - The Three Gorge Dam on the Yangtze River.

Our torque wrench has been widely used in oil fields, refinery and petrochemical industry,  power and hydropower plant, cement and construction, shipbuilding and repairs, mining and metallurgy, machine and equipments test and repairs.

Our customers including the most prominent corporation in China, such as China Petroleum, Sinopec, Baosteel, Jiangnan Shipyards, Conch Cement, China Metallurgical Group Corporation, and so on. The quality has been trusted by corporations and multinationals. Our products have been exported to North Ameica, South Ameica, Australia, and EU countries, quality is highly praised by our customers for reliability and durability.


Features:

 The outlet couplings and Allen key are able to rotate 360 °;
 Anti backlash design, prevent it the bolt to rotate with the nut;
 Easy-to-use and changeable square drive and Allen key to adapt different sockets;
 Output torque accuracy up to ±3%, can be customized for high torque;
 Hand free operation, efficient, and continuous working, drive pawl design for unrivaled accuracy and tool longevity;
 Optional sockets with different square drive to choose; one model can run 5-10 sets square drive sockets, tighten or dismantle different size of hexagon nuts and bolts;
 Corrosion resistant surface treatment to prevent corrosion and extend working life;
 Standard parts and accessories are available;
 Safe Design: Pressure relief valve prevents over-pressurization;
 Packed with aluminum tool case, user manual, safety handbook, calibration certificate, and each wrench is encoded with tracking system;
 Compatible to ISO9001:2008 and CE standard;
