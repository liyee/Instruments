About Us

Tarzan Instruments International Co., Ltd. was established in 2014 by a group of technicians specialized in hydraulic tools in London, UK. Ever since the establishment, we put quality, technology as our lifeline.
In 2017, we outsourced our manufacturing to Ningbo Instruments Mechanics Co., Ltd, a joint venture invested by TARZAN INSTRUMENTS INTERNATIONAL (UK). Since then, we have developed into a customers-oriented, technology-driven, quality-focused, multi-national, composed of R&D, marketing, distribution, and manufacturing.
